Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2024

Dewey is a book reading tracker app. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

üîí TL;DR: Your data stays on your device. We don't have servers, accounts, or analytics. We can't see your library.

üì± Information Stored on Your Device

Your Library: Books you add, reading progress, and lists are stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

üîç Book Search

When you search for books, we query the ISBNdb API to retrieve book information (titles, authors, covers, descriptions). Search queries are sent directly to ISBNdb. See ISBNdb's Privacy Policy for details.

‚ùå Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Advertising identifiers

We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your data.

üì∑ Camera Access

Dewey may request camera access to scan book barcodes (ISBNs). Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never stored or transmitted.

üóëÔ∏è Data Deletion

All your data is stored locally. To delete it, simply delete the app. There's nothing on our end to delete because we never had it.

üë∂ Children's Privacy

Dewey does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in tracking their reading.
